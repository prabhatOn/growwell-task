Introducing our comprehensive online blended learning program designed specifically for graduates, equipping them with essential skills in areas such as Banking, Financial Services, Capital Markets, Fintech, regulatory bodies, and more.

The primary objective of this program is to prepare participants for a career as Relationship Managers. As Relationship Managers, individuals play a pivotal role in adding value to both the bank and its customers. They possess the ability to identify and understand customers' financial needs, offering tailored solutions to meet those requirements.
